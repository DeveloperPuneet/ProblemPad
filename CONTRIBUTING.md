# ProblemPad Community Guidelines for Contributors

Welcome! To keep ProblemPad a helpful and friendly platform, please follow these basic rules when contributing:

## Be Respectful – Treat all users with respect. No harassment, offensive language, or personal attacks.

## Quality Content Only – Share accurate, clear, and relevant problems, solutions, or explanations. Avoid spam or low-effort content.

## Originality – Ensure your contributions are your own. Give credit when referencing others’ work.

## Stay On-Topic – Only post content related to ProblemPad’s purpose: problem-solving, learning, and discussion.

## Clear Formatting – Use proper formatting for readability (e.g., numbered steps, proper symbols, clean LaTeX/math formatting).

## Report Issues Responsibly – If you find mistakes, bugs, or inappropriate content, report them politely.

## Respect Privacy – Do not share personal information of yourself or others.
